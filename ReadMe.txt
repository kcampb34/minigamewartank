Title of Game: Tank War

Description: It's infinity against one!! Tank War has you play as the green army tank going against the red army tanks. Survive as long as you can by shooting back the infinitely spawning enemies. It's a one and done!! Don't by kill. Kill or be kill!! Good luck and may God be with you in this intense war game!!!!!!!!

Controls: Move with the arrow keys and shoot with the space bar

Dev Log: All army tanks and bullets were designed by me
